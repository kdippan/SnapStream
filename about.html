About SnapStream

Simplifying Web Capture for Everyone.

SnapStream was born out of a simple frustration: taking high-quality, full-page screenshots of websites shouldn't require expensive software, heavy browser extensions, or creating an account.

As developers and designers, we often need to archive landing pages, test responsive layouts, or generate assets for presentations. Existing tools were either too slow, watermarked, or trapped behind paywalls. SnapStream solves this by providing a free, fast, and privacy-focused utility accessible directly from your browser.

Our Core Mission

Speed First

We leverage edge computing and the Microlink API to render pages in milliseconds, not seconds.

Privacy Centric

We don't track your history. Images are generated on-the-fly and processed client-side.

The Technology Stack

SnapStream is a modern Single Page Application (SPA) built with:

  • Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+): For a lightweight, dependency-free footprint.
  • Microlink API: For enterprise-grade headless browser rendering (Chromium).
  • HTML5 & CSS3: For a responsive, accessible, and theme-aware UI.
